What are we?

We are a nonprofit organization focused on providing the community of the North Coast an improved, sustainable food system

Why are we important?

Doing your part to help save climate change can easily be done by your diet! We focus on community farms and ways in which individuals can access these viable options for theirs and also the planet's nutritional health,

How can we help you?

Where can you find Fresh, Organic, Local food within the North Coast BioRegion? We have the resources to help you find the right produce for your spedcial dietary needs!

Publications

We have produced 2 editions of our famous Locally Delicious Cookbook and also our LunchBox Envy. All of which have the ultimate goal of improving our food system.

Mission

"A recipe for change through the re-localization of our food system ~ one tomato at a time"

Locally Delicious is an incorporated, 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that supports the "re-localization" of the California North Coast food system and advocates for re-localization of food nationwide.

Goals

To improve the North Coast Food System

  • Educate about how to improve personal, community and planetary health through personal food choices
  • Create access to healthy, local, organic food for people at all income levels
  • Provide small grants to other community organizations with similar goals
  • Help to build a local sustainable food system

Locally Delicious, Inc.

is a non-profit organization engaged in education about the benefits of eating locally grown, organic food. We create and support projects designed to increase production of local organic food, with an emphasis on making good food accessible to all. We advocate for a healthy and sustainable local food system.
